Submission

Potential participants of SCCSE '26 are invited to submit contributions of the following category:

  • Full papers (8-10 pages), presenting original research work or providing insightful surveys of key developments on topics in the above list;

Full papers and progress reports should be written in English and prepared according to the following instructions. All submissions will be peer-reviewed by members of the SCCSE '26 Program Committee and external referees.

Paper submission instructions

  1. Sign up for an account on EasyChair.
  2. Join in the conference SCCSE 25 or SCAI 25.
  3. Create a new submission not a new version. Don't forget to add your name and number on Paper Title.
  4. You can see your paper on your menu after submission. By 'Submit a new version' on the top right corner, you can change your paper.
